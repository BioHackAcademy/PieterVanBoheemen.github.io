Welcome Biohackers!

In BioHack Academy you design, build, use and share your own biolab. Each edition connects several groups around the world passionate about biotech and hacking.

BHA4 is over and we are preparing for the Fifth Edition! Send an email to biohack@waag.org to connect with us or check the Waag BioHack Academy page to sign up.

The course materials remain on this website as a backup. You can start by taking a look at the Course Overview

Or join our BHA Facebook group

Partnerlabs BHA4

On April 4th the fourth BioHack Academy finished. The participating labs were

  • BioHack Western Australia - Perth
  • Waag Society's Open Wetlab - Amsterdam
  • M-Lab - Lithuania
  • BioClub Tokyo - Japan
  • National Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art - South Korea
  • Shenzhen Open Innovation Lab - China
  • The Social Media Workgroup, University of New Mexico - USA
