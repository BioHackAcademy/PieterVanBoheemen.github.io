## Mycelium

Strains:

* Fomes Fomentanius
* Piptoporus Betulinus
* Lenzites Betulina
* Pleurotus Ostreatus
* Trameles Versicolor
* Sparasis Crispa

Facts:

* Nice furry microbe, grows into strong fibers
* Grows best on [Malt Agar](/bha4/cultivation-media/malt-agar/)
* Find out more on [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mycelium)
