## Functional requirements

**Must have**

1. Sterile working area, about the size of 10 petridishes
2. Horizontal flow

**Nice to have**

1. Adjustable airflow

## Technical requirements

**Must have**

1. HEPA class H14 filter
2. 0.5 m2/h airflow for laminar flow
3. On/off switch

**Nice to have**

1. Power regulator
