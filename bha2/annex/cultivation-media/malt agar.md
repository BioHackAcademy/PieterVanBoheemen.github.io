## Malt Agar (MA)

Suitable for growing yeast and fungi.

|Compound| Amount | Unit |
|:-------|-------:|-----:|
|Malt extract|30.0|g|
|Agar|20|g|
|Demi water| up to 1000|mL|

Set pH ~5.5 

### Biohacker Alternative

* Malt extract can be easily made DIY style by boiling malt in water
